```
[Connection to brand pillar or differentiator].

Looking for [service type]? [CTA]

#hashtag1 #hashtag2 #localhash
```

## Platform-Specific Guidelines

**Facebook:**
- Longer captions okay (up to 250 words)
- Include link in post
- Use relevant hashtags (3-5)
- Post 3-5x per week

**Instagram:**
- Shorter captions (125-150 words)
- More hashtags (10-15)
- Story content daily
- Feed posts 3-4x per week

**LinkedIn:** (if applicable)
- Professional tone
- Longer thought leadership content
- 1-2x per week
