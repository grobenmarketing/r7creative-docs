# Photo & Video Guidelines

## Photo Requirements

**Technical Specs:**
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Minimum size: 720px x 720px
- Recommended: 1200px x 900px
- File size: Under 5MB
- No watermarks or promotional text

## Photo Categories Needed

**Cover Photo:** Signature image representing your business (1)
**Logo:** Square business logo (1)
**Exterior:** Building/signage photos (2-3)
**Interior:** Office/workspace photos if applicable (3-5)
**At Work:** Team performing services (10-15)
**Team:** Team member photos (2-5)
**Products:** If applicable

**Minimum Total:** 20 high-quality photos

---

## Video Requirements

**Technical Specs:**
- Maximum duration: 30 seconds
- Maximum file size: 100MB
- Format: MP4, MOV, AVI

**Video Ideas:**
- Business introduction
- Service process overview
- Customer testimonial
- Team introduction
