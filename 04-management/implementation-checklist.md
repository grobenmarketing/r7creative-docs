## Implementation Checklist (Detailed Steps)

### Pre-Strategy Phase

- [ ] **Contract signed**
- [ ] **Payment received**
- [ ] **Client CRM Account Fully Updated**
- [ ] **Welcome email sent**
- [ ] **ClickUp Client Folder Created**
- [ ] **JotForm configured**
- [ ] **Frame IO shared folder created**

### Brand Strategy Phase

- [ ] **Brand Strategy scheduled**
- [ ] **Brand Strategy completed**
- [ ] **Brand Pillars delivered** (within 2 days) — **TTV #1** (Time to Value #1)
- [ ] **Brand Pillars reviewed by client** (confirm they re...)

### Content Strategy Phase

- [ ] **Content Strategy scheduled**
- [ ] **Content Strategy completed**
- [ ] **First 9-12 content pieces assigned**

### Content Production & Launch Phase

- [ ] **First content scheduled**
- [ ] **First content published** — **TTV #2** (Time to Value #2)

### Success Review Phase

- [ ] **First Success Review completed** (2 weeks after publish)

---

## Key Milestones

### TTV #1: Brand Pillars Delivered
- **Timeline:** Within 2 days of Brand Strategy completion
- **Purpose:** First tangible deliverable showing value to client
- **Deliverable:** Brand Pillars document

### TTV #2: First Content Published
- **Purpose:** Client sees their content live in the market
- **Timeline:** After first 9-12 content pieces assigned and scheduled
- **Review:** First Success Review scheduled 2 weeks post-publication

## Meeting Types

| Meeting Type | Purpose | Timing |
|--------------|---------|--------|

| Onboarding | Brand Strategy Meeting | After contract signed |

| Value Review | First Success Review | 2 weeks after first content published |
| Sync | Bi-Weekly Sync | Ongoing every 2 weeks |

## Notes

- The checklist ensures systematic onboarding with clear milestones
- Two "Time to Value" (TTV) moments are built into the process to demonstrate early wins
- The structure supports both initial onboarding and ongoing client management
- Each phase has dependencies that ensure proper sequencing of activities
