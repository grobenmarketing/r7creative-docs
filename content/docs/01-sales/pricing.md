# Pricing Structure Summary
**Last Updated:** November 7, 2025

## Overview

R7 Creative uses a **two-tier pricing disclosure strategy**:

1. **External/Customer-Facing**: Simplified 3-tier pricing for website and customer materials
2. **Internal/Company-Facing**: Full 5-tier structure with add-ons and bundles for sales and operations

---

**One-Time Activation Fees for Website Services**

- **Founder:** $300 one-time
- **Retail:** $600 one-time

### Website: LaunchPad
- **Founder:** $1,450 one-time
- **Retail:** $1,950 one-time
- **Includes:** Standard onboarding + basic website setup (3-5 pages), Hugo framework implementation, Netlify hosting setup, GBP mirror integration
- **Monthly Credits:** 30 credits/month for ongoing updates
- **For:** Clients who need basic website presence with light maintenance

### Website: Authority Builder
- **Founder:** $2,750 one-time

- **Includes:** Standard onboarding + comprehensive website (10-15 pages), advanced Hugo framework, full SEO optimization, blog setup, sortable gallery, FAQ system

- **For:** Clients who need content-heavy sites with frequent updates and full authority positioning

**Note:** Website activation includes setup of Two Clocks update system for ongoing maintenance. Clients with LaunchPad or Authority Builder ALWAYS have a You Capture We Create content package (website services are add-ons to content, never standalone).

## Important Notes

### Founders Pricing
- **First 15 clients only**
- Lifetime lock-in (never increases)
- After 15 clients, new clients pay retail

### No Contracts
- All packages month-to-month
- Cancel anytime
- No long-term commitments

### 30-Day Free Trial
- Full service for 30 days at no cost
- Invoice day 23, payment due within 7 days (Net 7)
- Can decline payment and part ways if not satisfied
- Keep all content created during trial

### Media Capture Discount
- 15% discount on quarterly packages
- Quarterly: $600 vs. $750 (3 one-time sessions at $250 each)

## External vs. Internal Price Mapping

**Why the difference?**

External 3-tier structure simplifies customer decision with most popular options:
- **Social Starter** = Base Tier 2 package
- **4-Minute Dominator** = Tier 4 bundled/adjusted
- **Local Market Leader** = Tier 4 + Both Add-Ons

Internal 5-tier structure provides:
- Granular options for different budgets
- Upsell path from basic (Tier 1) to premium (Tier 5)
- Flexibility in custom packages
- Clear add-on pricing
