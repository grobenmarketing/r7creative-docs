## 1. Services Provided

### 1.1 Service Description
R7 Creative will provide professional on-site media capture services ("Media Capture Service") to Client, including:

- Pre-capture planning and shot list development
- On-site professional content capture (photos and videos)
- Post-production editing and optimization
- Platform-specific formatting
- Caption writing
- Content delivery via Frame IO for client review
- Integration with existing content calendar and publishing workflow (if Client is subscribed to R7 Creative monthly package)

### 1.2 Service Package

**Select One:**

☐ **One-Time Session**
- Single capture session
- Produces approximately 1 month of content (8-12 videos, 20-30 photos)
- No commitment beyond this session

☐ **Quarterly Package (3 Months)**
- Three (3) capture sessions over three (3) consecutive months
- Each session produces approximately 1 month of content
- Sessions scheduled in advance for consistency

☐ **Annual Package (12 Months)**
- Twelve (12) capture sessions over twelve (12) consecutive months
- Each session produces approximately 1 month of content
- Best value pricing with long-term commitment

## 3. Service Delivery & Timeline

### 3.1 Capture Session Details

**Each capture session includes:**
- Duration: 1-2 hours on-site
- Location: [Client's place of business / specified location]
- Equipment: Professional cameras, lighting, and audio equipment provided by R7
- Participants: Client and/or designated team members

### 3.2 Timeline

**Per Capture Session:**
- Pre-capture planning: 5-7 days before session
- Capture day: [Scheduled date]
- Editing and production: 3-5 business days after capture
- Client review period: 48 hours for feedback
- Revisions (if needed): 1-2 business days
- Final delivery: 5-7 business days from capture date

**Publishing:**
- Content will be published according to Client's content calendar
- For clients with R7 Creative monthly packages: R7 will schedule and publish
- For standalone service clients: R7 will deliver edited content; Client is responsible for publishing

### 3.3 Deliverables

**For each capture session, Client will receive:**
- 8-12 edited short-form videos (30-90 seconds each)
- Multiple platform formats: Vertical (9:16), Square (1:1), Horizontal (16:9)
- 20-30 edited professional photos
- Captions written for each piece of content
- Platform-specific hashtag recommendations
- All content delivered via Frame IO for review and approval

## 4. Client Responsibilities

### 4.1 Scheduling & Availability

- Client agrees to be available for scheduled capture sessions
- Client will provide at least 48 hours' notice for any rescheduling requests
- Client will ensure designated team members are available if planned for participation

### 4.2 Location & Logistics

- Client will provide appropriate location access for capture sessions
- Client will ensure locations are tidy and ready for filming
- Client will obtain any necessary permissions for filming at the location
- Client will notify building security, staff, or other relevant parties of R7's presence

### 4.3 Preparation

- Client will review and approve shot list prior to capture session
- Client will prepare any requested props, materials, or products for filming
- Client will communicate any last-minute changes or concerns in advance

### 4.4 Review & Approval

- Client will review delivered content within 48 hours via Frame IO
- Client will provide clear, consolidated feedback for any revision requests
- Client will provide final approval before content is published

## 5. Cancellation & Rescheduling

### 5.1 Client Cancellation of Session

- **48+ hours' notice:** No fee, session will be rescheduled
- **Less than 48 hours' notice:** $50 rescheduling fee
- **No-show:** Full session fee charged, session must be rescheduled

### 5.2 R7 Cancellation of Session

- If R7 must cancel due to equipment failure, illness, or emergency, session will be rescheduled at no charge
- R7 will provide as much notice as possible

### 5.3 Weather Cancellations

- If outdoor shooting is planned and weather is unsafe, session will be rescheduled at no charge
- Indoor backup plans will be discussed during pre-capture planning

### 5.4 Package Cancellation

**One-Time Session:**
- No commitment; either party may decline future sessions

**Quarterly Package:**
- Client may cancel with 30 days' written notice
- Unused sessions are non-refundable
- If Client cancels after one session, remaining balance for quarter is still due

**Annual Package:**
- Client may cancel with 30 days' written notice
- Refund for unused sessions minus 20% administrative fee
- Example: If Client cancels after 4 sessions, Client receives refund for 8 sessions minus 20% of total annual fee

## 6. Revisions & Additional Services

### 6.1 Included Revisions

- First round of reasonable revisions: Included in service fee
- Second round of reasonable revisions: Included in service fee

**"Reasonable revisions" include:**
- Caption adjustments or rewrites
- Different photo selection from captured set
- Different video take selection from captured footage
- Minor video editing changes (trimming, re-sequencing)
- Text overlay or graphics adjustments

### 6.2 Additional Fees

**The following may incur additional fees:**
- Third+ rounds of revisions: $100 per additional revision round
- Major scope changes (requesting entirely different content than shot list): Custom quote
- Re-shoots due to client dissatisfaction (not due to technical failure): Custom quote
- Rush delivery (faster than standard 5-7 day timeline): $150 rush fee

### 7.1 Content Ownership

### 7.2 R7 Portfolio & Marketing Use

- R7 may use final edited content for portfolio, marketing, and promotional purposes
- This includes (but not limited to): website portfolio, social media, case studies, client testimonials

### 7.3 Third-Party Rights

- Client warrants that any branding, logos, music, or other materials provided for use in content do not violate third-party intellectual property rights
- Client is responsible for obtaining any necessary licenses or permissions for music or other copyrighted materials requested for use
