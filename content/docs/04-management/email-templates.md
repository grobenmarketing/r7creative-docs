# Email & Communication Templates

## Overview

This document contains all standard email templates used throughout the client journey, from initial welcome through ongoing communication.

---

## 1. Client Welcome Email

**When to Send:** Immediately after contract signed and payment received

**Purpose:** Welcome the client, set expectations, and outline next steps

**Subject:** Welcome to [Company Name]! Let's Get Started

**Template:**

```
Hi [Client Name],

Welcome to [Company Name]! We're excited to partner with you on [brief description of project/service].

[Opening paragraph - thank them for their trust, express enthusiasm]

Here's what happens next:

1. **ClickUp Access:** You'll receive an invitation to your dedicated client folder in ClickUp where you can track all project progress and deliverables.

2. **JotForm:** Please complete the client intake form we'll send separately. This helps us gather essential information about your brand, business, and goals.

3. **Frame IO:** You'll get access to a shared folder for all video and media collaboration.

4. **Brand Strategy Meeting:** We'll schedule this within [timeframe] to dive deep into your brand positioning, target market, and messaging.

What you can expect from us:
- Clear communication and regular updates
- [Specific deliverable timelines]
- A dedicated point of contact for all your questions

Next Step: Watch for an email from us to schedule your Brand Strategy Meeting.

Looking forward to creating amazing results together!

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Title]
[Contact Information]
```

## 2. Send Brand Pillars

**When to Send:** Within 2 days of Brand Strategy Meeting completion (TTV #1)

**Purpose:** Deliver the first major value milestone - the Brand Pillars document

**Subject:** Your Brand Pillars Are Ready! [Client Name]

**Template:**

```
Hi [Client Name],

Great news! Your Brand Pillars are complete and ready for review.

[Link to Brand Pillars document]

What's Inside:
- [Brief description of what Brand Pillars include]
- [Key components covered]
- [How these will guide content creation]

Please review the document and let us know if you have any questions or feedback. This foundation will guide all the content we create together.

Next Steps:
1. Review the Brand Pillars by [date]
2. Send us any feedback or questions
3. We'll schedule your Content Strategy Meeting for [approximate timeframe]

This is an exciting milestone - your brand now has a clear, documented foundation!

Questions? Just hit reply.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Title]
[Contact Information]
```

## 3. Content Strategy Meeting

**When to Send:** After Brand Pillars have been reviewed and approved

**Purpose:** Schedule the Content Strategy Meeting

**Subject:** Let's Plan Your Content Strategy - [Client Name]

**Template:**

```
Hi [Client Name],

Now that your Brand Pillars are finalized, it's time to plan your content strategy!

In our Content Strategy Meeting, we'll:
- Define your content themes and topics
- Identify the types of content that will resonate with your audience
- Create a content calendar framework
- Assign your first 9-12 content pieces

Please book a time that works for you: [Calendar link or time options]

The meeting will take approximately [duration] and will set us up to start creating content that drives real results for your business.

What to bring:
- Any specific ideas or topics you want to cover
- Examples of content you've loved (yours or competitors')
- Questions about the process

Looking forward to it!

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Title]
[Contact Information]
```

```
- Define your target market and customer avatar

- Develop your core messaging framework

This meeting is the foundation for everything we create together, so we want to make sure we get it right.

**Duration:** [Typically 60-90 minutes]

**Please book a time:** [Calendar link or specific time options]

- Any existing brand materials or guidelines
- Competitor examples (what you like and don't like)
- Your goals and vision for the next [timeframe]

**Before the Meeting:**
- Review the Brand Strategy Meeting Agenda (attached)
- Think about what makes your business unique
- Prepare any questions you have

Looking forward to diving deep into your brand!

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Title]
[Contact Information]
```
