# Service Agreements

## Overview

This document contains templates for the standard 4 Minute Marketing Service Agreement and the 30 Day Trial version. These agreements establish the terms, scope, and expectations for the client engagement.

---

## 4 Minute Marketing Service Agreement

### Header

```
4 MINUTE MARKETING SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of [DATE] ("Effective Date")

BETWEEN:

[COMPANY NAME] ("Service Provider")
[Address]
[Contact Information]

AND:

[CLIENT NAME/BUSINESS NAME] ("Client")
[Address]
[Contact Information]
```

- First content published

#### 2.2 Ongoing Service

- Content production: [frequency]

### 3. Client Responsibilities

#### 3.1 For "You Capture We Create" Model

Client agrees to:
- Capture content according to provided guidelines
- Upload raw content to Frame IO within [X] days of scheduled capture
- Provide [X] minutes of raw content per [time period]
- Maintain consistent capture quality and environment
- Notify Service Provider of any delays or issues

#### 3.2 For All Service Models

Client agrees to:
- Attend scheduled strategy and sync meetings
- Review and approve content within [24-48] hours
- Provide timely feedback via Frame IO comments
- Communicate any business changes or campaign needs
- Maintain valid payment method on file
- Grant necessary access to social media accounts and tools

#### 3.3 Approval & Feedback

- Client will review content within [24-48] hours of submission
- Feedback should be specific and consolidated
- [X] rounds of revisions included per content piece
- Additional revisions may incur additional fees
- Silence after [X] hours constitutes approval (if specified)

---

### 4. Service Provider Responsibilities

Service Provider agrees to:
- Deliver services as outlined in Section 1
- Maintain content quality and brand alignment
- Meet agreed-upon deadlines and turnaround times
- Provide responsive communication (within 24 business hours)
- Protect client confidential information
- Use industry-standard tools and best practices
- Monitor content performance and provide insights
- Recommend strategy optimizations

**Initial Commitment:** [X months] (if applicable)

**By Client:**
- [X] days written notice required
- All outstanding invoices due immediately

**By Service Provider:**
- [X] days written notice required
- Prorated refund of prepaid services (if applicable)
- Client retains all completed work

**For Cause:**
- Either party may terminate immediately for material breach
- Written notice specifying breach required
- Opportunity to cure within [X] days

#### 5.3 Post-Termination

Upon termination:
- Service Provider delivers all completed work
- Client receives access to raw files and content
- Outstanding payments due within [X] days

### 8. Content Ownership & Usage Rights

#### 8.1 Content Ownership

**Upon full payment:**
- Client owns all final approved content
- Client may use content for any business purpose
- Client retains content after agreement ends

**During service:**
- Service Provider retains content until payment received
- Service Provider may use content in portfolio (with client permission)

#### 8.2 Raw Materials & Source Files

- Client receives final exported content files
- Source files (project files, layers, etc.) available upon request
- Service Provider retains backup copies for [X months/years]

#### 8.3 Portfolio & Marketing Use

Service Provider may:
- [ ] Use client content in portfolio and case studies
- [ ] Name client as reference
- [ ] Share results and metrics (anonymized or attributed)

Client may opt out by written notice.

#### 8.4 Third-Party Assets

Content may include:
- Stock photos/video (licensed for commercial use)
- Music (properly licensed)
- Fonts and graphics (licensed appropriately)

Client is granted usage rights as permitted by third-party licenses.

### 9. Platform Access & Permissions

#### 9.1 Required Access

Client will provide Service Provider with:
- Social media account access (admin or editor level)
- Analytics access
- Any relevant business accounts (Google My Business, etc.)
- Brand asset repository access

#### 9.2 Security & Confidentiality

Service Provider agrees to:
- Maintain security of login credentials
- Not share access with unauthorized parties
- Use access solely for service delivery
- Notify Client immediately of any security concerns

---

### 10. Confidentiality

#### 10.1 Confidential Information

Both parties agree to:
- Protect confidential business information
- Not disclose to third parties without consent
- Use confidential information only for agreement purposes
- Return or destroy confidential information upon termination

#### 10.2 Exclusions

Confidential information does not include:
- Publicly available information
- Information independently developed
- Information received from third parties without confidentiality obligation

## 30 Day Trial - 4 Minute Marketing Service Agreement

### Modifications from Standard Agreement

The 30 Day Trial Agreement includes all terms from the standard agreement with the following modifications:

---

### Trial Period Terms

#### Duration
- Trial period: 30 days from Effective Date
- Extended onboarding timeline to maximize trial value

#### Trial Service Scope

**Trial Includes:**
- Expedited Brand Strategy Meeting
- Brand Pillars document
- Content Strategy Meeting
- [X] pieces of content (reduced from standard package)
- All content published during trial period
- Performance metrics and reporting
- Trial Success Review at end of 30 days

**Trial Limitations:**
- Reduced content volume: [X] pieces vs. standard [Y] pieces
- One strategy iteration included
- Standard revision rounds apply
- Limited to [specific platforms if applicable]

#### Trial Pricing

**Trial Fee:** $[REDUCED AMOUNT]
- Due upon signing
- Non-refundable once service begins
- Applied as credit toward first month if converting to standard agreement

#### Conversion to Standard Agreement

**At end of trial period:**

**Option 1: Convert to Standard Agreement**
- Client chooses standard service package
- Trial fee credited toward first month
- Standard pricing begins immediately
- No interruption in service

**Option 2: End Engagement**
- Client provides notice by day [X] of trial
- All completed work delivered
- No further obligation from either party
- Service Provider may offer alternative packages

**Default:** If Client does not provide notice by day [X], agreement automatically converts to month-to-month standard agreement at standard pricing.

#### Trial Success Metrics

During Trial Success Review, parties will evaluate:
- Content quality and brand alignment
- Working relationship and communication
- Performance metrics and early results
- Client satisfaction with process
- Fit for ongoing partnership

#### Early Termination of Trial

**By Client:**
- May terminate anytime with 48 hours notice
- No refund of trial fee
- Receives all completed work to date

**By Service Provider:**
- May terminate for cause (non-payment, breach)
- Prorated refund of trial fee
- Client receives all completed work

## Agreement Selection Guide

### Use Standard Agreement When:
- Client is ready for full commitment
- Client has validated the service need
- Standard pricing and terms apply
- Long-term partnership anticipated

### Use Trial Agreement When:
- New client wants to test service
- Client is risk-averse or uncertain
- Service is new to client's business model
- You want to demonstrate value before full commitment
- Promotional or introductory offer
