## Tone & Voice Characteristics

### Confident but not pushy
You speak from experience without lecturing. You explain why things matter without talking down to them. You know this works because you've seen it work repeatedly.

**Use phrases like:**
- "Here's what I've found..."
- "The reality is..."
- "In my experience..."

**NOT:**
- "You need to..."
- "You should..."
- "You must..."

### Practical and no-BS
You cut through the noise of marketing hype. You're honest about what works and what doesn't. You acknowledge their skepticism because you've heard it a thousand times and proven it wrong a thousand times. You don't oversell; you show proof.

### Education-focused
Your goal is helping them understand where their business actually is (invisible, losing leads) vs. where it could be (visible, flooded with leads). You're not trying to convince them to buy marketing; you're trying to help them see what they're already losing.

### Service-oriented
You talk about working *with* them, not *for* them. You acknowledge that most business owners are in a frenzy and can't add more to their plate‚Äîso you make it easy. You're solving the real friction (time and complexity), not asking them to solve it themselves.

### Human and authentic
This is **"Human Touch Marketing in an AI & Automated World."** You emphasize authentic, raw content over polished corporate marketing because that's what builds trust faster. You're approachable, practical, and real.

‚ùå Vanity metrics (followers, likes, impressions don't matter‚Äîphone calls do)
‚ùå Overpromising or hype (let the results speak)

## Objection Handling

### Using the 4-Minute Decision Framework

#### "My customers don't use social media"

**Response:**
> "John wasn't 'using social media'‚Äîhe was checking your Google profile, website, and social to see if you're still in business. Big difference. 81% of customers research businesses online before buying. They're checking for a pulse across all platforms, not engaging with content."

#### "I get business without social media"

**Response:**
> "You get the customers who call without checking. What about the Johns who eliminate you in browser tabs? That's 20-30% of potential customers choosing competitors‚Äînot because of price or quality, but because your website was outdated and you looked inactive online."

#### "Social media doesn't work for my industry"

**Response:**
> "John doesn't care about your industry. He cares that you look attentive and available across your Google profile, website, and social media when he needs you. This isn't about selling on social‚Äîit's about passing the trust check across all platforms so they call you."

#### "I don't have time for social media"

**Response:**
> "That's exactly why John eliminated you. You're proving his point about being too busy or inattentive. That's why our system works‚Äîyou do 10-15%, we do 85-90%. You're not 'doing social media'; you're capturing 10-20 minutes of content weekly. We handle the rest."

#### "This is too expensive"

**Response:**
> "It's $550-950/month for our Founders pricing (full price is $1200-$1800/month). If 100 people monthly search for your service and you're invisible, you're losing $50K+ annually in calls that go to competitors. Our annual Founders investment is $6,600-$11,400. #### "I'm not sure this will work for me"

**Response:**
> "That's exactly why we offer a 30-day free trial. Don't take my word for it‚Äîsee for yourself. Try it free for 30 days. If it works, you'll know. If it doesn't, you owe nothing. Most clients see the first call in weeks 2-4."

#### "I tried marketing before and it didn't work"

**Response:**
> "I hear that a lot. Most businesses tried DIY social (gave up after 3 weeks), hired cheap freelancers (no strategy, no results), built a website that doesn't convert, or worked with agencies that charged thousands with no ROI. ## Channel-Specific Messaging Guidance

### Social Media Posts (Your Own Channels)

**Content Style:**
- Lead with the problem or invisible loss
- Use the "John Story" framework to illustrate the 4-minute decision
- Show before/after of visibility (what Google looked like before vs. now)
- Use client results as proof
- Share authentic behind-the-scenes of how you help clients

**Example Post:**
> "100 people searched for HVAC companies in [City] last month. Only 2 businesses got called. The other 3 that showed up in search? Eliminated in 4 minutes because their social profiles looked dead. Don't be one of the 3. [CTA]"

**Tone:**
Conversational, direct, no fluff. Use "we," "your," "here's what happens." Short sentences. Clear value.

**CTAs:**
- "See if you're showing up" (free visibility audit)
- "Let's talk about your visibility" (discovery call)
- "Ready to test this?" (30-day free trial)
- "Download the 4-Minute Decision guide" (lead magnet)

---

### Website/Sales Pages

**Homepage Structure:**
1. **Hero:** "We Keep You in the 4-Minute Decision" with subhead explaining the problem
2. **Problem:** "Your competitors are getting calls that should be yours" (invisible loss)
3. **Framework:** Explain the 4-minute decision window with "John Story"
4. **Solution:** "You Capture, We Create" system overview
5. **Packages:** Winner ($550) vs. Dominator ($950) with clear feature comparison
6. **Results:** Specific client transformations (1-2 calls/month ‚Üí 3-4 calls/week)
7. **Proof:** Case studies, testimonials, before/after visibility screenshots
8. **Risk Removal:** 30-day free trial offer
9. **CTA:** "Try Free for 30 Days" (primary) or "Schedule Discovery Call" (secondary)

**Key Elements:**
- Address objections upfront (price, time commitment, "will this really work?")
- Emphasize the free trial as the risk-removal tool
- Focus on their relief when it works, not your features
- Show the ROI calculation (cost of invisibility vs. cost of service)

### Discovery/Sales Conversations

**Opening (First 2 Minutes):**
> "I'd like to ask you something. In the last month, how many people do you think looked at your business online but never called? Because I can tell you‚Äîit's probably 5-10x more than you think, and there's a very specific reason why they chose your competitor instead."

**Diagnostic Questions:**
- "When was the last time you posted evidence of current work?"
- "If someone Googled you right now, would they see recent activity?"
- "Are you losing jobs to competitors who aren't necessarily better, just more visible?"
- "How's your calendar looking 4-6 weeks out?"
- "What have you tried before for marketing, and why did it stop?"

**Education (Show the Invisible Loss):**
- Pull up their Google Business Profile, website, and social media live on the call
- Show them what customers see (dead profiles, outdated website, outdated posts)
- Walk through the 4-minute decision process: "Here's what John does..."
- Show them the math: "100 searches/month √ó 4 competitors = 25 calls you should be getting. But you're getting 1-2. That's $50K+ lost annually."

**Solution Presentation:**
- "Based on what you've told me, you're losing the 4-minute decision to competitors who simply show up more consistently across Google, their website, and social. The good news? This is fixable."
- Explain "You Capture, We Create" (10-15% them, 85-90% you) plus website optimization
- Walk through the Brand Pillars foundation (drives all content and website copy)
- Show timeline: Month 1 (first call), Month 2-3 (3-4 calls/week), Month 6+ (calendar booked out)

**The Close:**
> "Here's what I recommend: Let's test this for 30 days‚Äîfree. You'll see the phone start ringing. You'll get your first 'I found you on Google' call. Then you decide if this is worth continuing. Sound fair?"

> "On day 23, you'll get an invoice for $550 (or $950). If you want to continue, pay it. If not, just reply and we part ways on good terms. No risk to you."

**Handling Resistance:**
- If they hesitate: "What's your biggest concern about trying this?"
- If time is the issue: "That's exactly why this works‚Äîyou do 10-20 min/week, we do everything else."
- If skepticism is the issue: "You've been burned before. I get it. That's why I'm not asking you to trust me‚ÄîI'm asking you to test it for free for 30 days. Let the results speak."
