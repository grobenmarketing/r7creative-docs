# GBP Optimization Checklist

## Purpose
Complete checklist ensuring every element of Google Business Profile is optimized according to Google's guidelines.

**Reference:** See `/_reference/` for Google's official GBP ranking research

---

## Pre-Optimization Setup

### Access & Verification
- [ ] Business claimed in Google Business Profile
- [ ] Business verified (postcard, phone, email, or instant)
- [ ] Primary account holder identified
- [ ] Manager access granted to team members (if applicable)
- [ ] Two-factor authentication enabled on account

### Business Information Accuracy
- [ ] Business name exactly matches real-world business name
- [ ] Address is accurate and matches physical location
- [ ] Service area accurately set (if applicable)
- [ ] Phone number is local (not tracking number on GBP)
- [ ] Website URL correct and working
- [ ] Hours of operation accurate
- [ ] Special hours set (holidays, etc.)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

**Profile Violations (Can Get Suspended):**
- [ ] Verified: No keyword stuffing in business name
- [ ] Verified: No promotional text in business description
- [ ] Verified: No URLs in business description
- [ ] Verified: Business category accurate
- [ ] Verified: Address is real business location
- [ ] Verified: No PO boxes or virtual offices (unless allowed)
- [ ] Verified: Hours are accurate
- [ ] Verified: Phone number rings to business

**Optimization Mistakes:**
- [ ] Avoided: Using same description as competitors
- [ ] Avoided: Generic photos (stock photos not allowed)
- [ ] Avoided: Inconsistent business information across platforms
- [ ] Avoided: Neglecting Q&A section
- [ ] Avoided: Not responding to reviews
- [ ] Avoided: Ignoring attributes
- [ ] Avoided: Outdated photos or information
