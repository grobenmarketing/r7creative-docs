# GBP Posts Strategy

## Post Types

### What's New Posts
Standard updates about your business
- Project showcases
- New services
- Business updates
- Seasonal tips

### Offer Posts
Promotions (must include specific offer, dates, terms)
- Seasonal discounts
- Service specials
- Package deals

### Event Posts
Time-specific events
- Open houses
- Workshops
- Community events
- Seasonal business hours

## Post Best Practices

- Include high-quality photo with every post
- Write clear call to action
- Keep text concise (300 characters or less for What's New)
- Post 2-4 times per week minimum
- Use "Learn More" button to link to relevant page
