# Questions & Answers Guide

## Strategic Q&A Seeding

The Q&A section on GBP allows anyone to ask questions. Proactively seed this section with strategic questions that:
- Address common customer concerns
- Highlight differentiators
- Include keywords
- Preemptively answer objections

## Monitoring & Responding

- Enable notifications for new questions
- Respond to all questions within 24 hours
- Keep answers professional and helpful
- Use brand voice in responses
- Don't argue with negative or loaded questions
